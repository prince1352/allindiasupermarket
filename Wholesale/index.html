Wholesale Services

All India Supermarket Provides Wholesale Support Built for Food Businesses. We Supply Restaurants & Tiffin Providers with Quality Ingredients Across Edmonton

Restaurant Grocery Solutions

Running a high-volume kitchen comes with unique challenges. That’s why we supply restaurant partners with reliable access to bulk staples including lentils, rice, flour, cooking oils, chutneys, and masalas. Our curated product lineup is tailored to South Asian cuisine, featuring both core ingredients and specialty imports.

Freshness matters—so we source locally whenever possible, and provide consistent delivery schedules to avoid disruptions. We also offer seasonal products, frozen convenience items, and packaging materials for takeout operations.

Whether you operate a single outlet or a franchise, our team helps streamline procurement and inventory planning to keep your kitchen moving efficiently, meal after meal.

Wholesale for Tiffin Providers

Our wholesale services are tailored to the needs of tiffin businesses who prioritize home-style taste and nutritional value. We provide flexible quantity options for ingredients like basmati rice, whole spices, legumes, oils, and eco-friendly containers so you can deliver meals with confidence.

Working with tiffin providers across Edmonton, we understand the demand for consistency, freshness, and affordability. Whether you're serving 10 or 100 clients daily, we help simplify your supply chain so you can focus on your menu.

We also offer custom invoices, tax-friendly billing, and dedicated support for recurring orders. It's wholesale, without the hassle.
